About me

I currently live in Grodno. I like to draw in my free time and play computer games. I prefer drawing animals over humans, though I draw cats and dragons the best. I have a dark gray cat by the name of Oliver. He's very cute, but doesn't like being pet. At least, not by me. At least, I don't walk home alone; my friend Maria lives next to me, so we walk home together. My favourite lessons are probably biology and mathematics. I'm the oldest in my class because I got sent back a year. I got sent back because I couldn't speak russian very well when I came here, but I think my russian has gotten a lot better since then.
